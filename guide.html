How to Use Your IPTV Playlist

This guide will help you use your Live TV playlist URL in popular IPTV applications. Follow these simple steps to start streaming live TV instantly.

Step 1: Install an IPTV Application

Download and install any IPTV player app on your device. Some recommended apps:

  • IPTV Smarters (Android/iOS)
  • GSE Smart IPTV (Android/iOS)
  • Perfect Player (Android/Windows)
  • VLC Media Player (All platforms)

Step 2: Open the IPTV App and Add Playlist

Open the installed IPTV app and look for an option like "Add Playlist" or "Add URL". Usually, this is in the Settings or Playlist section.

Step 3: Enter Playlist URL

Use the following playlist URL to add channels to your app:

https://tnm3u.live/playlist

Paste the URL into the app when prompted. The app will load all available channels automatically.

Step 4: Start Watching

After adding the playlist URL, navigate to your channels list and select any channel to start watching live TV instantly.

Step 5: Optional Settings

You can customize the IPTV app settings such as:

  • Channel grouping and categories
  • EPG (Electronic Program Guide) integration
  • Favorite channels list
  • Video quality preferences

Following these steps will allow you to stream your Live TV playlist easily on any device using your preferred IPTV application.
