App Overview

FM Radio is a feature-rich mobile application designed to provide seamless streaming of FM channels globally. With a user-friendly interface and real-time updates, listeners can tune into their favorite stations, discover new content, and enjoy uninterrupted audio experiences.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To connect listeners worldwide with high-quality radio streaming that is accessible anytime, anywhere.

Vision: To be the leading mobile platform for FM enthusiasts, bridging cultures through music, news, and entertainment.

Key Features

  • Live streaming of hundreds of FM channels worldwide
  • Recently played stations for quick access
  • Custom filters by language and type
  • Custom volume control and player interface
  • Idle screensaver displaying "Now Playing" info
  • User-friendly interface with smooth transitions and animations
  • Minimal battery usage for longer listening sessions
  • Offline caching of station icons and metadata for faster loading

How to Use

  1. Open the app and select your favorite station from the home screen or channel overlay.
  2. Use the play/pause button to start or stop streaming.
  3. Adjust volume using the slider or device buttons.
  4. Filter channels by language or type using the dropdown menus in the overlay.
  5. Check recently played stations for quick access to your favorite channels.
  6. Let the screensaver activate when the app is idle for a visually appealing “Now Playing” view.

Supported Languages

FM Radio supports channels in multiple languages. Filter stations based on your preferred language, making it easier to find content that suits your interests. Common languages include:

  • English
  • Hindi
  • Tamil
  • Telugu
  • Bengali
  • Spanish
  • French
  • And more...
