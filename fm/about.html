About FM Radio

Welcome to FM Radio

FM Radio is your one-stop app to listen to live FM channels from around the world. Discover your favorite stations, browse by language or type, and enjoy uninterrupted streaming.

Features

  • Live streaming of multiple FM channels
  • Recently played stations for quick access
  • Filter channels by language and type
  • Custom volume control
  • Idle screensaver with Now Playing info
  • Lightweight, fast, and user-friendly interface
