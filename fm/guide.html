Welcome to FM Radio

This guide will help you explore all the features of the FM Radio app efficiently.

play_circle

Player Controls

Use Play / Pause, Next, and Previous buttons to control your music. Volume slider adjusts sound smoothly.

history

Recently Played

Your last 10 radios are saved automatically for quick access anytime.

list

All Channels

Tap the List Button at the bottom right to view all FM channels in a full-screen list.

filter_alt

Search & Filters

Quickly find stations by searching or filtering by Language and Type (Regional FM or 24/7 Live).

tv

Screensaver Mode

If idle while music is playing, a beautiful screensaver shows Now Playing info with big controls.

update

Automatic Updates

All channel data updates live from our database. No app update needed for new radios!
